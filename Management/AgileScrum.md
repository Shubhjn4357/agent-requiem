# Agile Scrum & Modern Project Workflows

## 1. Roles

- **Product Owner**: Defines the "What" (Product Backlog).
- **Scrum Master**: Facilitates the process and removes blockers.
- **Development Team**: Technical implementation.

## 2. Ceremonies

- **Sprint Planning**: Defining the goal for the next 2-4 weeks.
- **Daily Standup**: Brief sync on progress, plans, and blockers.
- **Sprint Review**: Showcasing work to stakeholders.
- **Sprint Retrospective**: Improving the team's process.

## 3. Artifacts

- **Product Backlog**: Master list of all desired features.
- **Sprint Backlog**: Tasks committed to for the current sprint.
- **Increment**: The "Done" usable software at the end of a sprint.

## 4. Estimation

- Use Story Points (Fibonacci sequence) to estimate effort rather than hours.
