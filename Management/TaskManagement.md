# Task Management & Atomic Deliverables

## Workflow Rules

1. **Atomic Updates**: Each tool call or group of calls should represent a complete, functional change.
2. **Sequential Progress**: Follow the `task.md` checklist in order. Mark items as `[/]` (in-progress) or `[x]` (completed).
3. **Documentation**: Update implementation plans if significant architectural shifts occur.

## Reporting Standards

- Always provide a clear, concise summary of changes.
- Avoid "filling" reports with fluff; stick to hard facts: _What changed?_ _Why?_ _How was it tested?_
- Do not stop for feedback mid-task unless a critical blocker is reached.
