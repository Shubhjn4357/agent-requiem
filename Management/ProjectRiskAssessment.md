# Project Risk Assessment

## 1. Goal

Identifying potential risks that could affect your project's timeline, budget, or quality.

## 2. Identification

Use various techniques to identify risks, such as brainstorming, interviewing subject matter experts, and reviewing historical documents.

## 3. Qualitative and Quantitative Analysis

Perform qualitative and quantitative analysis of each identified risk to assess its likelihood and impact.

## 4. Response Planning

Develop strategies to address each identified risk, such as risk avoidance, mitigation, or acceptance.

## 5. Monitoring and Review

Regularly monitor your risks and update your response plans as needed throughout the project.
