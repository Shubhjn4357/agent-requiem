# Risk Management

## 1. Identification

Identify potential risks that could affect your project, such as technical challenges, resource constraints, and external factors.

## 2. Assessment

Assess the likelihood and impact of each identified risk.

## 3. Mitigation

Develop strategies to mitigate each identified risk, such as contingency plans or risk avoidance.

## 4. Monitoring

Monitor your risks throughout the project and update your mitigation strategies as needed.

## 5. Communication

Communicate your risks and mitigation strategies to stakeholders.
