# Cloud-Native Design Principles

## 1. Scalability

Design your application to be easily scale up and down as needed.

## 2. Resilience

Design your application to be resilient to failures.

## 3. Managed Services

Leverage managed services like AWS Lambda, Azure Functions, and Google Cloud Functions to simplify development and management.

## 4. Automation

Automate your entire software delivery process, from testing to deployment.

## 5. Security

Implement security at every layer of your application and infrastructure.
