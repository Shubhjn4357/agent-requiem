# User Acceptance Testing (UAT)

## 1. Goal

UAT is the final stage of testing before releasing your application to production. It's conducted by end-users to ensure the application meets their requirements and expectations.

## 2. Planning

Define a clear UAT plan that includes the test scenarios, acceptance criteria, and participant roles.

## 3. Execution

Provide participants with the necessary training and support to conduct their UAT.

## 4. Feedback and Issue Resolution

Actively seek feedback from UAT participants and address any identified issues.

## 5. Decision Making

Use the results of UAT to determine whether the application is ready for release.
